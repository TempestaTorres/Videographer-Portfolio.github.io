About James

Experience

James is an CAA and FAA certified videographer with over 10 years of experience in his field.

He has worked with many brands to create promotional videos and commercials centered around nature and adventure.

Education

A bachelor's degree in photography, and visual arts. He can provide a comprehensive understanding of photography concepts, techniques, and equipment.

Skills

Some of his skills include, but are not limited to:

  • Manage time efficiently and deliver content by deadline
  • Work harmoniously with Creative director or collaborative team
  • Know what type of equipment to use for your individual project
  • Shooting high quality video footage both indoors and outdoors
  • Aerial videography: licensed drone operator
  • Setting up equipment, troubleshooting problems and making necessary repairs so projects can go forward even in more desolate locations
  • Good communication skills and the ability to interview people
  • Flexibility and highly adaptive to different situations
  • Patience to wait for the perfect shot
